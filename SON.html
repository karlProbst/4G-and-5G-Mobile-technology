SON

Category: 4G / radio
Executive Summary:
Self-Organizing Network (SON) is an automation suite that dynamically configures, optimizes, and heals network parameters. It drastically reduces manual engineering and operational overhead.

Advanced Technical Definition

Crucial for managing complex HetNet topologies, SON algorithms provide automated self-configuration, self-optimization, and self-healing. When a newly deployed eNodeB (eNB) or 5G gNB is powered on, SON automatically integrates it into the Evolved Packet System (EPS), dynamically adjusting RF parameters, tilt, and power levels to minimize interference via ICIC without human intervention.
