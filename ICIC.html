ICIC

Category: 4G / radio
Executive Summary:
Inter-Cell Interference Coordination (ICIC) mitigates interference at the edges of cellular coverage. It orchestrates intelligent frequency allocation between neighboring base stations.

Advanced Technical Definition

ICIC suppresses signal degradation at cell boundaries by instructing adjacent eNodeB (eNB) stations to transmit at orthogonal frequencies or drastically different power levels. Executed mostly via FFR, ICIC is critical in dense HetNet deployments, actively protecting micro/pico cells from the high-power macro cell signals and ensuring continuous throughput at the Evolved Packet System (EPS) boundaries.
