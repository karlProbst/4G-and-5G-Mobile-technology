5G-NR

Category: 5G / radio
Executive Summary:
5G New Radio (5G-NR) is the unified, highly capable air interface standard for 5G networks. It modulates signals across a massive spectrum ranging from sub-6 GHz to mmWave frequencies.

Advanced Technical Definition

Standardized from 3GPP Release 15 onwards, 5G-NR utilizes scalable OFDM numerology to heavily support massive MIMO, 3D beamforming, and Multi-RAT internetworking. In a Non-Standalone (NSA) setup, the 5G-NR carriers rely on the 4G Evolved Packet Core (EPC) for signaling. In Standalone (SA), 5G-NR interfaces natively with the gNB and the 5G Next Generation Core (NGC).
