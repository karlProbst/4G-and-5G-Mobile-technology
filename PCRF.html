PCRF

Category: 4G / architecture
Executive Summary:
The Policy Control and Charging Rules Function (PCRF) manages QoS and billing in the 4G network. It enforces subscriber plan limitations and prioritizes traffic.

Advanced Technical Definition

Housed inside the Evolved Packet Core (EPC), the PCRF dynamically tracks data consumption and applies service tier rules based on statistical multiplexing. It interworks with the MME and gateways to throttle, drop, or prioritize packet flows originating from the eNodeB (eNB). It is strictly required to enforce QoS parameters for latency-sensitive applications, ensuring compliance with delay-bound violations.
