CoMP

Category: 4G / radio
Executive Summary:
Coordinated Multi-Point (CoMP) allows multiple base stations to jointly process and transmit signals to a single device. It essentially transforms edge-cell interference into useful, additive signal gain.

Advanced Technical Definition

CoMP utilizes advanced spatial techniques including Coordinated Scheduling (CS), Coordinated Beamforming (CB), Joint Transmission (JT), and Dynamic Point Selection (DPS). Multiple eNodeB (eNB) units coordinate tightly over X2 interfaces to send synchronized data simultaneously to the UE, significantly boosting edge throughput and mitigating interference within 4G and LTE-Advanced architectures.
