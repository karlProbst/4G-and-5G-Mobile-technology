HetNet

Category: 4G / radio
Executive Summary:
Heterogeneous Networks (HetNet) interweave large macrocells with numerous small cells (pico, femto). This multi-layered architecture dramatically increases overall capacity in dense areas.

Advanced Technical Definition

A HetNet overlays low-power, small-range nodes onto the overarching coverage area of a traditional macro eNodeB (eNB). Managing the resulting chaotic RF environment and spectrum reuse requires robust Self-Organizing Network (SON) algorithms, ICIC, and CoMP. It is absolutely essential for managing the high-density requirements (e.g., 1 million devices/km²) of 5G and modern LTE-Advanced deployments.
