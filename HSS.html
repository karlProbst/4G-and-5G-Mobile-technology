HSS

Category: 4G / architecture
Executive Summary:
The Home Subscriber Server (HSS) is the central 4G database containing user subscriptions and security keys. It replaces the fragmented 3G HLR/AuC components.

Advanced Technical Definition

The HSS acts as the master cryptographic and profile database within the Evolved Packet Core (EPC). It executes mutual network authentication—ensuring the UE trusts the network and vice versa—by feeding EPS AKA vectors directly to the MME. This guarantees that traffic entering the E-UTRAN and passing through the eNodeB (eNB) belongs to validated subscribers with precise QoS profiles.
