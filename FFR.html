FFR

Category: 4G / radio
Executive Summary:
Fractional Frequency Reuse (FFR) partitions the frequency spectrum intelligently across cell sectors. It physically ensures neighboring cells do not clash on the identical channels.

Advanced Technical Definition

Deployed logically within the E-UTRAN, FFR allocates a central, low-power frequency block for inner-cell users (who already have high SNR) and distinct, higher-power frequency blocks for edge users. It acts as the core mathematical enforcement mechanism for ICIC, orchestrated continuously by the eNodeB (eNB) to maintain clean, interference-free OFDMA subcarriers.
