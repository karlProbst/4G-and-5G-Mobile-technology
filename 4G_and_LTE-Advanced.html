4G and LTE-Advanced

Category: 4G / architecture
Executive Summary:
4G and LTE-Advanced represent the fourth generation of mobile networks characterized by an all-IP routing architecture. It achieved true 4G ITU compliance by reaching gigabit speeds through advanced antenna technologies and spectral efficiency.

Advanced Technical Definition

Based on 3GPP Release 10 and beyond, LTE-Advanced leverages the Evolved Packet System (EPS) to deliver true gigabit broadband.

StandardTarget DownlinkTarget Uplink
LTE (Rel 8)300 Mbps75 Mbps
LTE-A (Rel 10)3 Gbps1.5 Gbps


It integrates robust Carrier Aggregation (up to 5x 20MHz bands) and high-order MIMO (up to 8x8) to push spectral efficiency to 30 bps/Hz. The system interconnects seamlessly with the Evolved Packet Core (EPC) and utilizes dense eNodeB (eNB) arrays within the E-UTRAN to manage high-speed IP traffic.
